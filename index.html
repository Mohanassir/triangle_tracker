Triangle tracker program

Basically, this is an application program that allows the user to input the lengths of the sides of a triangle, and returns whether these sides form an equilateral, isosceles or scalene triangle or if the sides cannot make a triangle at all

What is a triangle

A triangle basically is a figure that has three sides and three angles

Types of a triangle

Here is a list of the types of triangles

  • Equilateral triangle - It is a triangle where all sides are equal
  • Isosceles triangle - It is a triangle where two sides are equal
  • Scalene triangle - It is a triangle where all sides are not equal
